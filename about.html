Our Story

Kardhan Engineering has been at the forefront of providing high-quality industrial equipment and engineering solutions to leading organizations across India.

With years of experience in the aerospace, research, and engineering sectors, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality, and customer satisfaction. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted partner for prestigious institutions and organizations.

We specialize in supplying a comprehensive range of products including bearings, lifting equipment, electrical & electronics, HVAC systems, safety equipment, and industrial machinery to meet the diverse needs of our clients.
